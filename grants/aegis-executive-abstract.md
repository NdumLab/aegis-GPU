# Aegis Executive Abstract

## 150-Word Version
Aegis-GPU is a browser-based AI infrastructure incident training platform that helps learners and early-career operators build practical troubleshooting judgment across GPU hardware, CUDA stack, networking, storage, and orchestration scenarios. Unlike static labs or content-only training, Aegis emphasizes evidence-first reasoning, layer identification, action safety, and consequence-aware decision-making. The platform currently includes guided incident labs, reasoning scorecards, study and quiz surfaces, branch-aware troubleshooting flows, and browser-proof validation artifacts that support pilot readiness. We seek grant support to move Aegis from a validated prototype into a measurable pilot for workforce-development and technical training settings. A pilot would serve learners preparing for AI infrastructure, SRE, platform engineering, and support roles, with outcomes measured through diagnostic accuracy, unsafe-action reduction, clean incident completion, quiz performance, and time to correct diagnosis. Aegis is positioned as a credible pilot-ready training platform, not yet as an externally ranked or independently validated market leader.

## 75-Word Version
Aegis-GPU is an interactive training platform for AI infrastructure troubleshooting. It helps learners practice evidence-first diagnosis across GPU, CUDA, NCCL, storage, scheduler, and networking incidents, with reasoning scorecards and branch-aware learning flows. We seek pilot support to turn Aegis from a validated prototype into a measurable workforce-training implementation, with outcomes focused on diagnostic accuracy, action safety, clean incident completion, and troubleshooting readiness for emerging AI infrastructure operators.
